Frequently Asked Questions

What's Notion?

Notion is an incredibly powerful platform that makes it accesible for everyone to manage databases and documents. You can find out more about it on their website. P.s. I even set up my personal site using Notion.

Why don't you offer money back?

Notion doesn't offer ways to properly restrict access to their shared templates, so once you get my template you can duplicate it right away and own it, therefore there's no way for me to control if by getting your money back you will indeed not use my product. Sorry.

Why is this better than other options?

As I tackle on my case study, most of the tools made specifically for filmmakers are specialized in one area of the production, which means you end up having multiple files across multiple services. With Notion you can have most of them in one place and collaborate with the entire crew..

How do the 1:1 calls work?

If you need help understanding how the dashboard works and how to make it work for more specific needs you and your crew might have, I am available to make a call with you to solve the issues that can be solvable. Notion isn't straightforward to understand for someone who's never used it, so I can help ease out the adaptation process.

Why pay for this?

I dedicated more than 70 hours doing this dashboard, writing instructions, tips, tricks and more. I am a graduated filmmaker as well, so I know what I am talking about. By paying you're saving yourself a lot of hours of setting up a dashboard from scratch, I already did it and the price benefit compared to the hours spent is incredible.
